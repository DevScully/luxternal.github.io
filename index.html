What is Luxternal® Giveaways? Luxternal® Giveaways is a South African Social Media Marketing agency. Our goal is to bring mass awareness to companies and people, through targeted large-scale influencer giveaways.
How does sponsoring Luxternal® Giveaways Work? Sponsoring Influencer giveaway's works as follows:

  1. Several Influencers promote the rules of Luxternal Giveaways through their instagram feed & instagram stories.
  2. Rules will always instruct celebrity / influencer followers to go to @luxternalgiveaways_za and follow everyone we follow to be entered into the giveaway.
  3. We aim to keep a limited spot of high-quality Sponsors, to ensure that there will be maximum participitants and maxmium growth oppertuntiy for our Sponsors.

What types of people enter the Luxternal® Giveaways?The entrants of each Giveaway vary depending on the Influencer promoting the particular Giveaway. With that being said, Luxternal® Giveaway focuses entirely on South African Giveaways, where the only Entrants that are allowed to be entered into the Giveaway are residents of South Africa and 18+ years of age.
How long are the Giveaways? Our giveaway schedule will be announced in detail on our Instagram story @luxternalgiveaways_za.
What Sponsors are best suited for our giveaways? Our services are suited to indivduals & businesses that have a similar target audience to our Celebrity / influencers. This will have to be an active Instagram account whos goal is to bost & develop their personal brand, increase sales and reach their content to a large sample of their target audience.
How do i become a sponsor? You can sponsor our giveaway by clicking the "sponsorship" tab, where you can choose an upcomming celebrity giveaway and sign the contract & offically purchase a spot as a Sponsor.
